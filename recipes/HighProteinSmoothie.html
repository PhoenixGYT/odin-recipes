Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Home

Description

This is a high protein smoothing containing peanut butter and bananas to meet protein goal while also being refreshing.

Ingredients

  • Choice of milk
  • Choice of nut butter(here peanut butter would be used)
  • Bananas
  • Honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  4. Enjoy!
